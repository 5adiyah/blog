From there, I started looking more into design and got into web design. In the last two or so years I took some courses on HTML and CSS and made several websites with that knowledge. I'm currently enrolled in the Epicodus bootcamp where I will be studying for the next couple of months. I enjoy trying out new recipes, listening to podcasts, playing around with photoshop, and catching up on my favorite shows.
